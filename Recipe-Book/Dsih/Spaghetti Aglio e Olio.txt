Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Ingredients

200g spaghetti

3 garlic cloves (sliced)

3 tbsp olive oil

1 tsp chili flakes

Salt to taste

Fresh parsley (chopped)

Preparation Steps

Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente.

In a pan, heat olive oil and sautÃ© garlic until golden.

Add chili flakes, drained spaghetti, and parsley.

Toss well and serve hot.
